# Contributing to B20 Mortgage

Thank you for your interest in B20 Mortgage! We welcome educational contributions, bug reports, and suggestions.

## Guidelines

1. **Educational Purpose**: Keep code clear, clean, and well-commented.
2. **Branding**: Follow the minimal, institutional brand voice.
3. **Commit Messages**: Use conventional commits (`feat:`, `docs:`, `test:`, `chore:`).
4. **Disclaimers**: Ensure that any contract modification keeps the disclaimer section in tact.

For any questions, contact us at **support@b20mortgage.com**.
