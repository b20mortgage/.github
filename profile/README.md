> **Tokenization of real-world assets — real estate, vehicles and beyond.**

### The Vision
We believe in the institutional, minimal, and fully compliant transition to on-chain assets. As traditional finance converges with Web3, the **tokenization of all assets** is not just an opportunity; it is an inevitability. B20 Mortgage is building the foundational primitives to bridge crypto liquidity directly into real-world assets (RWA) with institutional-grade standards, clean mechanics, and robust engineering.

---

### Featured Repositories

* 🏢 **[b20-real-estate-tokenization](https://github.com/b20mortgage/b20-real-estate-tokenization)**
  Solidity smart contracts for tokenizing real estate. Features property NFT deeds, fractional ERC-20 ownership, compliance registries (KYC/AML), an escrow-based marketplace, and a rental income distribution engine.
  * *Stack: Hardhat + TypeScript + Ethers.js v6 + OpenZeppelin v5*

* 🚗 **[b20-vehicle-tokenization](https://github.com/b20mortgage/b20-vehicle-tokenization)**
  Solidity smart contracts for tokenizing vehicles and movable assets. Features VIN-bound NFTs, lien management, P2P escrow transfers, on-chain vehicle leasing, and valuation oracles.
  * *Stack: Foundry (Forge/Cast) + Solidity*

* 📚 **[b20-tokenization-research](https://github.com/b20mortgage/b20-tokenization-research)**
  Comprehensive research papers, legal & compliance frameworks, and multi-language SDK examples (TypeScript, Python, Go) for interacting with tokenized assets.
